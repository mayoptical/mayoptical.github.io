About

At May Optical, we have been providing quality eyewear to the Toronto community for over 25 years. Originally opened by Paul Lau in 1985 on Dundas and Spadina, we are now located in Scarborough on Silver Star Blvd and Steeles Ave. We have a great selection of unique designer frames, sunglasses, contact lenses and more. We strive to meet each customer’s visual needs with our experience, knowledge and our everyday great prices.
